• Press T to turn on Translate mode (the default). Click and drag the mouse to move the object in the x and y directions. Scroll the mouse wheel to move the object in the z direction.
• Press R to toggle Rotate mode. Click and drag the mouse to rotate the object.
• Press A to toggle Ambient Light mode. Scroll the mouse wheel to increase or decrease the ambient light intensity.
• Press D to toggle Directional Light mode. Scroll the mouse wheel to increase or decrease the directional light intensity. Click and drag the mouse to move the directional light source.
• Press B to toggle the Background.
